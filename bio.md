## Bio

I'm an evolutionary biologist interested in testing fundamental theories about adaptation. I grew up in the French Alps near Geneva, then moved to London (UCL) for my undergraduate and postgraduate education. I did my PhD studies in the UCL Department of Genetics, Evolution and Environment (GEE), working on the genetics of sexually antagonistic variation in *Drosophila melanogaster*, under the primary supervision of Prof. Max Reuter. For my postdoctoral research (2019-present), I moved to the Monash School of Biological Sciences (Melbourne), where I collaborate with Dr Tim Connallon to address a variety of questions about adaptation.
