## Bio

I'm an evolutionary biologist interested in fundamental theories about adaptation and tests of such theories. I grew up in the French Alps near Geneva, then moved to London (UCL) for my undergraduate and postgraduate education. I did my PhD studies in the UCL Department of Genetics, Evolution and Environment, working on the genetics of sexually antagonistic variation in *Drosophila melanogaster* under the primary supervision of **Prof. Max Reuter** (2015-18). For my postdoctoral research (2019-present), I have been based in the Monash School of Biological Sciences (Melbourne), where I collaborate with **Dr Tim Connallon** to address a variety of questions about the genetics of adaptation.
